Why Your Support?

Bethlehem Telephone Sanitarium is a community committed to serving humanity in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ. Our ministry is dedicated to providing technical services that address the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of individuals. Jesus came to give us health, peace, and perfection of character, and our mission is to continue His work by bringing healing and restoration to those in need.

Your donation today will help to make a one-time purchase of the property you see in the images in these pages, which will be a permanent home for this mission. This facility - located on 18 hectares of land - will be more than just a church; it will be a comprehensive center for lifestyle healing, cooking training, counseling, and spiritual nourishment.

Facility Features

  • Worship Space: A dedicated area for worship services, where people can come together in faith.
  • Healing Foods Kitchen: A specialized kitchen where we will conduct workshops and classes on preparing healing foods that promote health and wellness.
  • Counseling and Seminar Spaces: Rooms for short-term health and counseling seminars aimed at holistic well-being.
  • Accommodation: Comfortable lodging for participants attending our seminars and workshops, allowing them to fully immerse themselves in the experience.

Why be healthy?

We need health to be alive and effectively listen, and respond positively to the work of the Lord. If we are unwell, we may not be able to take the message to the whole world effectively.

In Matthew chapter 28:18-20, Jesus said, "All authority has been given to Me in heaven and on earth. Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things that I have commanded you; and lo, I am with you always, even to the end of the age."

In light of the above, we need health not necessarily to enjoy or be proud about it, but to be able to work as per the commission given by Jesus. Jesus came for us all and every human being has an equal opportunity to be saved and anyone who calls upon Jesus in faith will be saved.

It is my and your responsibility to know and tell all nations about Jesus, and what He is saying. For that purpose, we need life and health to be able to effectively live by, and take that message to people. The recipients also have to be alive and healthy, to be able to accept to hear, and interpret that message correctly, then make the right choice of obeying and living according to the ten commandments of God. All other good reasons for health may be added benefits.

It is very important then to understand from the very beginning that, there are laws governing health, and that we are responsible to bring our individual life practices into harmony with those laws. Thus is the main purpose of Bethlehem Telephone Sanitarium Inc.
